Mighty Breakfast Chipotle Sausage Tacos

Description

These Mighty Breakfast Tacos take breakfast to the next level. With chipotle sausage, streaky bacon, Cheddar and smashed avocado, this is a breakfast to remember.

Ingredients

  • 6 rashers streaky bacon
  • 20 g chipotle paste
  • 6 plain taco tortillas
  • 225 g pork and oregano sausage meat
  • 60 g mature cheddar cheese
  • 1 pot smashed avocado
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C/200°C fan/gas mark 7.

    Lay the bacon in a single layer on a lined baking tray.

    Once the oven is hot, bake on the middle shelf of your oven until golden brown and crispy, 10-15 mins. IMPORTANT: Wash your hands and equipment after handling raw meat. Cook bacon thoroughly.

  2. Meanwhile, heat a drizzle of oil in a large frying pan on medium-high heat.

    Once hot, add the pork sausage meat and fry until browned, 5-6 mins. Use a spoon to break it up as it cooks. IMPORTANT: Wash your hands and equipment after handling raw meat. It's cooked when no longer pink in the middle.

    Once the sausage meat is cooked, add the chipotle paste and cook for a further 1 min, then transfer to a small bowl and cover to keep warm. TIP: Keep the fat in the pan to fry your eggs.

  3. Whilst the sausage cooks, grate the cheese.

    In a medium bowl, whisk the eggs until combined and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  4. Return the (now empty) frying pan back to medium-high heat with a drizzle of oil.

    Once hot, pour in the whisked eggs and cook for 30 secs without stirring, then gently stir, making sure the eggs don't stick to the bottom of the pan, 2-3 mins. Once cooked, remove from the heat. IMPORTANT: Ensure egg whites are fully cooked.

  5. When the eggs have 2 mins remaining in the pan, pop the tortillas onto a baking tray and into the oven on the top shelf to warm through, 1-2 mins.

  6. To build your breakfast tortillas, lay them flat on a board, then place a bacon rasher in the middle of each.

    Arrange the scrambled eggs, chipotle sausage meat and a dollop of smashed avocado on top.

    Carefully transfer the tortillas to 2 serving plates and sprinkle over the grated cheese to finish.

    Enjoy!
